Transparent Pricing for Quality Legal Services

At John Wilson Law Firm, we believe in clear, upfront pricing so you can make informed decisions about your legal representation. Our fee structure is designed to provide flexibility while ensuring you receive the expert counsel you deserve.

All fees are subject to VAT at the current rate. For complex cases, we may recommend alternative fee arrangements such as fixed fees or conditional fee agreements (no win, no fee) where appropriate.
